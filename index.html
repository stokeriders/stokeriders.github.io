SERVICES

KITEBOARDING

Catch the stoke off the shores of Lake Michigan's many kiteboarding beaches with kiteboarding, SUP-kiting and tandem boarding.

KAYAKING

Comfortably paddle on along the shore by yourself or with another beach buddy in one of our tandem kayaks.

PADDLEBOARDING

Get your stoke by paddling on a calm day, surfing the rolling waves or by Stand Up Paddleboarding(SUP) Yoga.

SNOWKITING

Get your stoke without traveling to the slopes. Kite through the winter on snowcovered fields and lakes with ski's or a snowboard.

LANDKITING

Have the need for speed or just want to improve your kite skills. The kitebuggy will get you stoked for land, snow or water.
